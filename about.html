Our Mission

Mission Statement

Metro Consulting helps Bronx-based small business owners grow, streamline, and enhance their company. We make this possible by enlisting smart and motivated Fordham students from a variety of disciplines to volunteer as consultants. Our goal is to make a positive impact on our local community while simultaneously giving students hands-on experience in the real world.

How We Do It

Metro consultants (students) are placed in a team and assigned a client (a Bronx-based small business) to work with for the school year; these teams are determined based on each student’s interests and experience as well as the needs. Through bi-weekly meetings between the team and client, deliverables are created by combining the owner’s vision and consultants’ recommendation.

Metro Consulting’s partnership with businesses provides a learning experience for both sides: consultants can give advice based on their knowledge and experiences, and clients help students understand the inner-workings of business ownership. Previously, consultants have proven to be adept in improving social media development and client communications, but other potential deliverables.
